Bill Gates

Here is a timeline of Bill Gates' life:

  • 1955: Born on October 28, in Seattle, Washington.
  • 1972: Co-founded Traf-O-Data with Paul Allen and Paul Gilbert.
  • 1975: Dropped out of Harvard with support from his parents.
  • 1976: Registered "Microsoft" in New Mexico.
  • 1980: Microsoft partnered with IBM to develop an operating system.
  • 1985: Introduced the first version of Microsoft Windows.
  • 1987: Began dating Melinda Gates after meeting at a Microsoft event.
  • 2007: "Graduated" from Harvard by receiving an honorary degree.
